1. Overview

We respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, or email) on our own servers. Our applications are designed to process data locally on your device whenever possible.

2. Third-Party Services

To maintain, improve, and monetize our applications, we use trusted third-party services. These services may collect certain technical data or anonymous identifiers:

  • Google Firebase We use Firebase for Usage Analytics and Crashlytics. This helps us understand how the app is used and fix crashes. Data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Google AdMob We display ads to support the free version of the app. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier to show relevant (personalized) or generic ads, depending on your iOS App Tracking Transparency settings.
  • RevenueCat We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat processes anonymous purchase receipts generated by Apple to validate your "Premium" status. They do not have access to your banking information.
